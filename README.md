# HSR MotorHub Assignment

## Product Name
HSR MotorHub

## Overview
HSR MotorHub is a desktop-first lead management platform designed for the sales team and business managers at HSR Motors. It centralizes lead capture, qualification, follow-up, and pipeline tracking, while providing real-time collaboration and automation.

## Screen 1 - Lead Listing
Purpose: Provide a searchable, filterable list of all incoming leads.

Key elements:
- Search input for leads by name, company, email, or phone.
- Filters for status, source, and assigned agent.
- Action cards for automation features like auto-assign, follow-up reminders, and lead quality.
- Table view with lead contact, source, status badge, assigned sales rep, and created date.
- Bulk actions such as export CSV and quick assignments.

## Screen 2 - Lead Details
Purpose: Show a complete profile and activity timeline for a selected lead.

Key elements:
- Lead profile header with name, role, company, score, and current status.
- Contact details and company information.
- Next action card that prompts the sales rep with the next recommended activity.
- Timeline feed for calls, notes, status changes, and internal comments.
- Note entry input for logging activity quickly.

## Screen 3 - Lead Management
Purpose: Manage lead stages with a pipeline board for qualification and follow-up.

Key elements:
- Kanban-like board with columns for New, Contacted, Interested, Not Interested, and Converted.
- Lead cards that display the lead name, company, email, phone, and time added.
- Board settings and ability to add stages.
- Visual counts in each stage for quick pipeline health checks.

## Screen 4 - Dashboard
Purpose: Give business managers an at-a-glance view of lead performance and team progress.

Key elements:
- KPI cards for total leads, conversion rate, active campaigns, and estimated revenue.
- Automation panel for smart follow-up, auto lead scoring, and campaign pulse.
- Area chart showing lead volume vs. conversions across the week.
- Recent activity panel for the latest assignment and team action updates.

## Workflow
1. Leads arrive from digital platforms and offline campaigns.
2. The sales rep uses the Lead Listing screen to review and qualify new leads.
3. The rep clicks into Lead Details to update status, log notes, and follow recommended next actions.
4. The Lead Management board is used to move leads through the funnel and keep the pipeline organized.
5. The Business Manager uses the Dashboard to monitor performance, identify risks, and see automation recommendations.

## Automation Features
- Auto-assign leads to sales reps based on workload and lead source.
- Follow-up reminders for leads that have not been contacted in 48 hours.
- Lead quality scoring that highlights high-priority opportunities.
- Campaign pulse alerts for performance drops in active campaigns.

## Mockup Description
The user interface uses a modern desktop layout with:
- A left sidebar for navigation.
- A top navbar for search and user actions.
- Clean cards and rounded containers for metrics, actions, and status.
- Strong visual hierarchy with color-coded status badges and automation panels.
